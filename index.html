Brief Biography 

Jun Hu received his B.S. degree in computer science from AnHui Normal University in 2011. From 2011, he is a Ph.D Student of School of Computer Science and Engineering in NanJing University of Science and Technology and a member of Pattern Recognition and Bioinformatics Group, led by professor Dong-Jun Yu.
