Brief Biography 

Mr. Jun Hu received his B.S. degree in computer science from AnHui Normal University in 2011. From 2011 to 2018, he acted as a Ph.D Student of School of Computer Science and Engineering in NanJing University of Science and Technology and a member of Pattern Recognition and Bioinformatics Group, led by professor Dong-Jun Yu. From 2016 to 2017, he acted as a visiting student at the University of Michigan (Ann Arbor) in U.S.A, led by professor Yang Zhang. He is currently a common academical and educational worker in the College of Information Engineering at Zhejiang University of Technology. His current interests include bioinformatics, pattern recognition, machine learning, and data mining. Homepage in ZJUT: http://www.homepage.zjut.edu.cn/jun_csbio
